    # TradeFlow System Deep Analysis

## 1. System Overview

TradeFlow is a highly sophisticated, institutional-grade automated paper trading system that integrates multi-strategy technical analysis, Large Language Model (LLM) orchestration, and robust dynamic risk management. 

### Core Architecture
- **Trading Engine (`src/server/background-runner.ts`):** A centralized orchestrator that polls Binance websockets (`5m` candles and `24hr` tickers) and handles state reconciliations.
- **Strategy Engine (`src/strategy-core/*`):** A multi-factor strategy pipeline evaluating 30+ strategies grouped into distinct orthogonal "Families" (e.g., trend, momentum, volatility).
- **Fusion Engine (`src/strategy-core/fusion/index.ts`):** Synthesizes strategies into a `StrategySnapshot`. It utilizes an Herfindahl-Hirschman Index derived `effectiveN` to dynamically decorrelate cluster bias.
- **AI Orchestration (`src/server/ai-decision.ts` & `src/services/ai/orchestrator/pipeline.ts`):** Groq-powered LLMs act as cognitive arbiters. The models do not *run* the strategies; they *evaluate* the Strategy Snapshot, resolving conflicting signals and assigning confidence.
- **Risk System (`src/lib/risk/*`):** Continuous volatility targeting and drawdown gating scale the position sizes to normalize risk exposure across disparate market regimes.
- **Dynamic Trade Management (`src/services/trade-manager/*`):** Open positions are managed dynamically, bypassing static Take Profit (TP) / Stop Loss (SL) levels. The system adjusts levels based on "Trade Health", R-multiples, trailing stops, and early AI exits.

## 2. Full Trade Lifecycle

1. **Market Data Ingestion:**
   - Binance WebSocket streams `5m` klines and tickers into the `BackgroundRunner` cache.
2. **Strategy Evaluation (`runStrategyPipeline`):**
   - Calculates shared technical indicators (EMA, RSI, ATR, MACD, WaveTrend, Keltner, etc.).
   - Evaluates 30+ deterministic strategies. Each strategy outputs a signal (`BUY`/`SELL`/`HOLD`), a confidence score, and momentum/trend scores.
3. **Fusion & Consensus (`fuseStrategies`):**
   - Clusters strategies by `StrategyFamily`.
   - Computes `effectiveN`, `familyNetDirection`, and `familyAlignmentScore`.
4. **AI Validation (`getStrategyDecision`):**
   - The LLM receives the `StrategySnapshot` alongside `CandlestickIntelligence` and sentiment data.
   - The LLM arbiter outputs a `MarketDecision` with its own `confidence` and `setupQuality` (A+, A, B, C).
   - If the LLM is rate-limited, the system falls back to a deterministic `localFallbackDecision` engine.
5. **Risk Gating & Sizing (`computeRiskAdjustedSize`):**
   - Applies volatility-targeting (ATR% relative to 1.5% target).
   - Applies peak-equity drawdown scaling.
   - Sizes the trade based on account balance and `setupQuality`. Rejects trades if constraints fail.
6. **Execution & Persistence (`createPaperOrderInternal` -> `fillPaperOrderInternal`):**
   - Creates a pending order and fills it atomically in a Prisma transaction, reserving `marginUsed` against the `PaperWallet`.
7. **Dynamic Management (`runAiTradeManagerLoop` -> `evaluatePosition`):**
   - A 30s background loop evaluates open positions using the `HealthScorer`.
   - Triggers `PARTIAL_EXIT`, `EARLY_EXIT`, `TRAIL_SL`, `ADJUST_TP`, or `BREAKEVEN_SL` based on real-time metrics.
8. **Exit:**
   - The trade hits a dynamic SL/TP, or the LLM commands an `EARLY_EXIT`.
   - PnL is atomic-swapped back into the `PaperWallet`.

## 3. Strategy Engine

The strategy engine executes parallel evaluations of independent anomalies. It fundamentally relies on the concept of **Orthogonality Clusters (Families)** to prevent false consensus.

### Family-Aware Logic
Strategies are tagged by descriptive `StrategyCategory` and clustered by latent `StrategyFamily` (`trend`, `reversion`, `volatility`, `structure`, `sentiment`, `arbitrage`, `ml`).

**The Problem Solved:** Legacy weighted voting double-counts the trend factor whenever the market is trending. If 10 trend-following strategies fire, they historically registered as 10 independent votes. 

**The Solution:**
- `familyNetDirection`: The mean of signed weights within a family. 10 trend strategies act as *one* vote at their mean conviction.
- `effectiveN`: Calculated as `(Σw)² / Σw²` across family absolute contributions. It measures the true number of independent signals driving the consensus.

### Clustering and Selection
The engine evaluates strategies matching the current `MarketRegime`. Strategies self-report their `regimeFit`.

## 4. Confidence System

Confidence is computed at multiple layers:
1. **Strategy Confidence:** Base confidence (0-100) generated by the individual deterministic rule.
2. **Weighted Score:** Strategy confidence is multiplied by a `regimeWeight` (0-1) depending on how well the strategy fits the detected market regime (e.g., trend strategies get suppressed in choppy markets).
3. **Family Alignment Score:** The percentage of total weight aligned with the dominant signal, clustered by family.
4. **LLM Confidence:** The LLM evaluates the snapshot and asserts final confidence.
5. **Dynamic Confidence (`calculateDynamicConfidence`):** Once in a trade, confidence decays based on consecutive adverse candles, ATR compression, and MACD divergence.

## 5. Take Profit / Stop Loss Engine

TradeFlow abandons static TP/SL for a living, breathing risk perimeter.

### Initial Calculation
- Without LLM override, default SL is calculated via `ATR * 2` (or dynamic percentage).
- TP targets `SL * 1.8` (RR 1.8).

### Dynamic Updates (`dynamic-levels.ts`)
- **Break-even Stop:** Triggered if PnL reaches a fraction of the TP distance OR if trade health decays into the 50-59% band. SL moves to entry `± (ATR * BREAKEVEN_BUFFER_ATR_MULT)`.
- **R-Multiple Profit Protection:**
  - `+1R`: Locks in 0.25R.
  - `+2R`: Locks in 1.0R.
  - `+3R`: Locks in 2.0R.
- **Trailing Stop:** Activates once price moves `1.5 * ATR` in profit. The trailing distance scales with regime: `4.0x` for volatility, `3.5x` for trend, `2.0x` for chop. It tightens for late-stage trends (>25 candles).
- **TP Reduction:** If `Trade Health` drops below threshold (e.g., 35), TP distance is aggressively reduced by up to 40% (`MIN_TP_MULTIPLIER = 0.6`).
- **Safety Guards:** Enforces monotonic tightening (SL can only tighten, never widen). Adjustments are clamped to `15%` of the remaining distance per cycle to prevent noise stop-outs.

## 6. Dynamic Trade Management

A 30-second interval loops over open LLM positions to assess them via the `TradeManager`.

### Exit Intelligence (`exit-intelligence.ts`)
The AI generates an aggregate confidence score for an `EARLY_EXIT` by weighting the following signals:
- EMA Death/Golden Crosses (weight: 0.25)
- Regime Shift (weight: 0.20)
- MACD Momentum Collapse (weight: 0.15)
- Candlestick Reversals (weight: 0.15)
- Bollinger Bands Breakdowns (weight: 0.15)
- RSI Breakdowns (weight: 0.10)
- Volume Climax against the trade (weight: 0.10)
- Critical News Risk (+25% confidence boost)

If exit confidence >= 70, it triggers an early exit. It requires *candle confirmation* (e.g., 3 consecutive adverse candles, scaled by volatility) unless overridden by a `LIQUIDATION_MOVE` or critical news.

## 7. Risk Management System

Risk is normalized across the portfolio before a trade executes via `computeRiskAdjustedSize`.

### Volatility Targeting
Normalizes position sizes against a target daily volatility (1.5%). 
```typescript
multiplier = TARGET_VOL / FORECAST_VOL (ATR%)
```

### Drawdown Scaling
Reduces risk exposure when equity dips below its high-water mark.
```typescript
drawdownPct = (peakEquity - currentEquity) / peakEquity
multiplier = Math.pow(1 - (drawdownPct / maxAllowedDrawdown), exponent)
```

### Size Calculation
- **Base Risk:** Maps `setupQuality` to risk percentages (A+ = 1.5%, A = 1.0%, B+ = 0.75%, B = 0.5%, C = 0.25%).
- **Notional Cap:** Enforces max equity % per trade (e.g., 50% for A+).
- **Output:** `riskAmount = totalEquity * baseRisk * volMult * ddMult`.

## 8. AI / LLM Orchestration

The LLM (Groq: `llama-3.3-70b-versatile` or `qwen3-32b`) acts as the final arbiter.

### Orchestration Mechanics
- **Queueing & Throttling:** Requests are scheduled round-robin per active symbol to respect API limits.
- **Fail-Fast Degradation:** If the premium model is exhausted (429) or unavailable, it degrades to mid-tier (`qwen3-32b`), then to instant tier, dynamically selecting available API keys.
- **Local Fallback Rescue:** If all keys/models fail, the `localFallbackDecision` deterministic engine takes over. It reduces maximum size caps to 25% and tightens thresholds (requires `alignmentScore >= 70` + `netDirection >= 25` for execution).
- **News Validation:** LLMs ingest structured news sentiment and output an `aggregateClass`. A `CRITICAL_RISK` rejection completely vetos the trade.

---

## 9. Regime Detection

The regime engine uses ADX, ATR, and moving averages to classify market states:
- `Trending Up` / `Trending Down` (ADX > 25, MA slopes aligned)
- `Choppy` (ADX < 20, frequent MA crosses)
- `High Volatility` (ATR expansion)

Regime directly scales:
- **Strategy Weights:** Trend strategies get a `regimeWeight` of 0 in `Choppy` markets.
- **Trailing Stops:** Trail multipliers range from 4.0x (Volatile) to 2.0x (Choppy).

## 10. Telemetry & Analytics

- **Explainable Signals:** Every LLM decision, whether executed, rejected, or shadowed, is persisted to `ExplainableSignal`. This includes the exact technical variables, candlestick intelligence, reasoning, and JSON execution results.
- **Trade Management Events:** Dynamic level adjustments and partial exits are audited into `TradeManagementEvent` to reconstruct the living trade path.
- **Correlation Tracking:** `computeRollingCorrelations` asynchronously tracks strategy cross-correlations to ensure `effectiveN` calculations remain valid over time.

---

## 11. Database & Persistence

- `PaperWallet`: Tracks realized `balance` and `usedMargin`.
- `PaperPosition`: Stores perpetual futures-style positions. Records `entryPrice`, live `quantity`, and `status`. Employs optimistic concurrency (`updateMany` with CAS logic) to prevent double-settlement.
- `ExplainableSignal`: Retains LLM telemetry for dashboard auditing.

## 12. Frontend / UI Logic

- Next.js server actions stream live PnL and telemetry to the client.
- The UI polls `getManagementEvents` to reconstruct visual timelines of how a trade's SL/TP shifted from its `originalTakeProfit` origins to its eventual close.

## 13. Current Weaknesses

- **Polling Over-reliance:** The `BackgroundRunner` checks conditions strictly on standard loop intervals. A sudden intra-interval tick relies completely on the next tick processing.
- **Backfill Latency Gap:** The system attempts to catch offline SL/TP breaches by scanning REST backfilled candles on startup. If an extreme wick happened during downtime, it's executed retroactively at the exact SL, ignoring slippage.
- **Over-parameterization Risk:** The dynamic levels engine introduces extensive `if/else` ladders for R-multiples and Health Scores. This density is prone to edge-case clashes under extreme volatility.

---

## 14. Current Strengths

- **Institutional-Grade Fusion:** The implementation of `effectiveN` to suppress correlated signals is a massive leap over standard weighted-average retail bots.
- **LLM Safety Abstractions:** The LLM does not execute direct code; it acts as a structured JSON arbiter. The local fallback engine ensures uptime.
- **Living Trade Management:** The transition from static TP/SL to health-based, trend-age-aware dynamic adjustments accurately mimics professional desk risk management.

---

## 15. Final System Assessment

TradeFlow represents a highly mature, production-focused paper trading system. Its architecture flawlessly blends deterministic quantitative analysis with qualitative LLM reasoning. The execution realism (isolated margin logic, atomic settlements) and statistical realism (vol-target sizing, decorrelated fusion) mark it as an institutional-grade platform capable of surviving real-world market noise.
